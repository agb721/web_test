Origin Story

From Mezcal & Surgery to Ideation & Product

How does a mezcal tasting, a shoulder injury, and a hernia surgery lead to a simple yet powerful technological solution?

Andreas (programmer) and Benjamin (musician) met at a "Philosophical Mezcal Tasting" in Mexico City. They bonded over their shared love for climbing and soon started working on an app that combined climbing with music.

Only a few months later, Andreas had a hernia surgery, and learned that some surgeons struggled with ergonomics while operating. So he adapted the app to help surgeons-in-training with their posture.

Along the way, he learned that the product could be expanded to help with physiotherapy, pilates, and other fields.
